Ragdoll. History

Cajun Ragdolls is a small cattery originally located in Westlake, Louisiana, with beautiful docile ragdoll cats and kittens loved by Anne Paul's family. After historic Hurricane Laura and Hurricane Delta devastated our beautiful home, we returmed to my hometown of Houston, Texas. We are very proud of our champion ragdolls, which are exhibited at the International Cat Association across the United States and have showcased some top titles/awards in the CFA. I am the CFA Ragdoll Breed Committee, if anyone has questions or concerns about ragdolls, please feel free to call 713-429-9150. Check out the cats page for our cats and kittens on the show.
